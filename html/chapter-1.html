From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamental vegatable or kitchen garden ,Often flowers ( edible and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegatable to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much foe their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A woll-designed portager can provide food,cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. potager can disguise their function of providing for a home in
a wide array of forms-from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
